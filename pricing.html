Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through your account dashboard. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle.

Is there a limit on meeting recordings?

Starter users can record meetings locally. Business users get 5GB of cloud storage, and Enterprise users enjoy unlimited cloud storage for all their sessions.

What happens after the 40-minute limit on Starter?

On the Starter plan, meetings with 3 or more participants will end at 40 minutes. You can restart the meeting immediately or upgrade to Business for unlimited duration.
